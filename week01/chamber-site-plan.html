Overview

Site Name

Sankofa Chamber of Commerce

The name is taken from the popular Ghanaian adinkra symbols. Being a community dominated by Ghanaians and other Africans, the name unifies the people culturally. Additionally, the name teaches the wisdom of learning from the past to build for the future.

Purpose

The Sankofa chamber of commerce serves as a representative for businesses in the adenta community. The organization helps promote businesses the organization of fairs and conferences. In addition, we serve as an advocate for small scale businesses in the area. This Website will serve as a means for people to recieve information and an easy for potential business owners to join the chamber.

Audience

Big, small business and potential business owners within the community; visitors, tourists and all other persons who may be interested in learning more about the businesses and business opportunities in the community.

Scenarios

1. What are some benefits of join the chamber for local business owners?
2. Which kind of events does the chamber organize with the aim of promoting business in the area?
3. Where can I find a list of members and tourist locations as a new resident in the community?
